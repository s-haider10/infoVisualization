Usage Scenario


Raj is an Indian college student studying in China really interested in learning about the rapidly-growing Indian economy. He wants to learn what primary factors are contributing to this rapid fiscal growth year-by-year. Also he recently learned that India is now the most populated country in the world, and now he is wondering whether population growth and its other demographics have contributed or not to India’s growth. Hence when Raj logs into the system, he will see a heatmap covering information about India’s population growth and GDP growth in the recent years. The heat map would show how in different provinces population growth and GDP growth were experienced. An overall scatter plot chart and bar chart graph would also be presented to amplify the understanding and the economic relevance of these two variables. Smart interactions will also be added in the heatmap (see below) for Raj to see how different provinces in India performed during that time.

India is a diverse country with 28 states.
